|----------|----------|----------|

### CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst)

<span className="difficulty-badge difficulty-advanced">고급</span>

글로벌 최고 권위의 금융 자격증

#### Level I

| 토픽 | 가중치 | 연계 모듈 | 커버리지 |
|------|--------|----------|----------|
| Ethical Standards | 15-20% | - | 별도 학습 |
| Quantitative Methods | 6-9% | C1, A4 | 85% |
| Economics | 6-9% | F4 | 70% |
| **Financial Statement Analysis** | **11-14%** | **F1, F2** | **95%** |
| **Corporate Issuers** | **6-9%** | **C1, C3** | **90%** |
| **Equity Investments** | **11-14%** | **C1, C2, A1** | **95%** |
| Fixed Income | 11-14% | A4 | 90% |
| Derivatives | 5-8% | A4 | 85% |
| Alternative Investments | 7-10% | A2, A3 | 80% |
| Portfolio Management | 8-12% | F4, M3 | 85% |

#### Level II

| 토픽 | 가중치 | 연계 모듈 | 커버리지 |
|------|--------|----------|----------|
| **Equity Valuation** | **10-15%** | **C1, C2, C3, M1** | **95%** |
| Fixed Income | 10-15% | A4 | 90% |
| Derivatives | 5-10% | A4 | 85% |
| Alternative Investments | 5-10% | A2, A3, C4 | 85% |

**핵심 연계 내용 (Level II Equity)**
- Industry and Company Analysis
- [Discounted Dividend Valuation](/valuation/dcf/overview)
- [Free Cash Flow Valuation](/valuation/dcf/fcff-fcfe)
- [Market-Based Valuation](/valuation/relative/overview)
- [Residual Income Valuation](/valuation/residual-income)
- Private Company Valuation

**추천 학습 경로**
1. 전체 기초 트랙 (F1-F4)
2. 전체 핵심 밸류에이션 트랙 (C1-C4)
3. [A4. 채권 및 파생상품](/curriculum#a4-채권-및-파생상품-25시간)
4. [거장 트랙](/curriculum#거장-트랙-40시간) (특히 그레이엄, 버핏)
5. [실전 트랙](/curriculum#실전-트랙-40시간)
